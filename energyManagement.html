Energy Audits and Management

Our Energy Management Team help our customer to conduct Audit by an Empanelled Accredited Energy Auditor (EMAEA), in accordance with the Bureau of Energy Efficiency Regulations, 2010.

Maven offers Consultancy/Training Services to get the certificates followed by the BEE guidelines in energy intensive sectors such as Cement, Fertilizer, Textile, Pulp & Paper, Iron & Steel, Chlor-Alkali, Petroleum Refineries, Thermal power plants and Railways.

We also suggest/implement or assist the methods for reduction in energy consumption complying the norms notified by EC Act, 2001 and assist in trading of Energy Savings Certificates (ECerts).

We also offers modular training Programs For Senior, Middle And Shop Floor Level Executives.
